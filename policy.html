Unit Policies

Policy Letter I - Welcome

i. The Command Staff would like you to know that our office is not one of power, but of responsibility. It is our duty to steer the course of this unit to greatness. That said the power and might of this unit is YOU! We can not do great things without you and your dedication. We look forward to you and the talent you can bring to this group.

Policy Letter II - Command Philosophy

i. Within Task Force Spartan, we believe highly in personnel following the in-place chain of command. Our front line of leadership are the FTLs and SLs within the unit. We believe in the idea that such personnel are in the prime position to assist with and deal with any issues that may arise during a member's time with TFS. With this, it is expected that any issues within the unit are first given to a member's immediate leadership personnel, who will if necessary, continue the issue further up the chain-of-command. While every member values the opinion of every other member, regardless of rank, we want to ensure that those within the lower levels of leadership are as active with the average members as possible, and are the next link in the chain to higher command.
